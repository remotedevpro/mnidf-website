About MNIDF

MNIDF (Muassaat Nasrul ilm wa Da'wah Foundation) is a forward-thinking non-governmental organization committed to driving sustainable community development, empowering individuals, and accelerating digital transformation across underserved communities.

We bridge the gap between potential and opportunity through education, digital innovation, and impactful social programs that foster growth and long-term development.
